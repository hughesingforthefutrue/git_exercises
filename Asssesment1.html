OC, Maryland. The Coolest?!

History:

Land was owned and founded by Thomas Fenwick.Back in the early years of foundation, may east coast vistors went to OC, MD for fishing, soak in the sun and beach, and to collect seashells. That with the land being an undeveloped land of sand, being different then what it is today. In 1952 and 1964 respectively, the Chesapeake Bay Bridge and Chesapeake Bay Bridge Tunnel were completed, allowing access to the resorts that were being built in the late 80's. Tourism exploded in the 70's with expansions on high-rise condminium buildings. 80s-90s OC started to engage on beach erosion projects that help replenish beach fronts on the dune line.
